## Name
PageWatch - Web Page Change Monitor

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Monitor any webpage for changes. Get instant alerts for price drops, restocks, new posts, and keywords. Free Discord/Slack webhooks.

## Detailed Description

PageWatch automatically monitors web pages and alerts you the moment something changes. No more manually refreshing pages — set it and forget it.

**What can you monitor?**
- Price drops on product pages
- Restock alerts when sold-out items become available
- New job postings on career pages
- Blog or news updates
- Any keyword appearing or disappearing on a page

**Key Features:**
- One-click setup with preset templates (Price Drop, Restock, Job Posting, Announcement, Keyword Watch)
- Smart conditional alerts — only get notified when YOUR conditions are met (keyword appears, price drops below target, etc.)
- Free webhook notifications to Discord, Slack, Telegram, or any custom endpoint
- Visual diff highlighting — see exactly what changed
- CSS selector support — monitor specific sections of a page
- Element picker — click to select which part of the page to watch
- Change history with full before/after comparison
- CSV export for data analysis
- Runs silently in the background — works even when the page isn't open

**How it works:**
1. Add any webpage URL or click "Monitor Current Tab"
2. Optionally pick a specific element to watch
3. Set your alert condition (any change, keyword, price threshold)
4. PageWatch checks automatically at your chosen interval
5. Get desktop notifications + webhook alerts when changes are detected

**Free Plan:**
- Up to 3 monitors
- 30-minute check interval
- 5 recent changes

**Pro Plan ($4.99/month):**
- Unlimited monitors
- Check every 1 minute
- Full change history
- CSV export
- Priority support

Perfect for deal hunters, job seekers, investors, researchers, and anyone who needs to stay on top of web content changes.

**Privacy:** PageWatch works entirely locally. Your data stays in your browser. No external servers, no tracking, no accounts required.
